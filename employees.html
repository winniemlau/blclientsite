At Belmar, we CARE about all of our employees and are proud to offer the following benefits:


HEALTH PLANS

• Medical: Major Medical, Wellness and Basic plans to choose from
• Dental
• Vision
• Term Life

We are proud to offer you choice, enabling you to pick the plan that best meets your health care needs while staying in compliance with the Affordable Care Act (ACA). Have questions? email benefits@belmarservices.com


RETIRMENT PLAN

Use our 401k savings plan to put aside money on a tax-deferred basis for your retirement. Eligibility at 20 1/2 years of age and 1 year of service. The plan allows for monthly enrollment. Voya (www.voyaretirementplans.com) will send a welcome packet out to associates once you reach eligibility. If you have any questions you should contact Voya at 800-584-6001.


REFERRAL BONUSES

Current employees may be eligible for a referral bonus of an amount determined by your facility manager after the person you referred works a minimum number of hours.


PAYCARDS

All of our employees are paid or have the option to be paid through our pay card system. No need to wait for your paycheck or rush to the bank. You can access your funds, at least one time free of charge (each week), via a national network of ATMs, local banks and point of purchase machines. Your payroll funds can also be directly deposited to the bank account of your choice through our paycard program.


PAY STUBS AVAILABLE ONLINE

Your paystubs are accessible online so you can access them 24/7.


TRAINING AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Tutorials are available for the most popular software plus we offer specific training and video aided job preparation which you may participate in.


EMPLOYEE TRANSFERS

Your employment records and work history will be transferred to any of our offices or divisions. Your accrued work hours will transfer and are immediately applicable to the programs available in your new location.


WELLBEING

Visit www.livesolid.com for guidance on your wellbeing which includes information on how to manage your money, relationships, retirement, family and your future.


AT&T WIRELESS PHONE SERVICE

1. Go to att.com/wireless/employbridge
2. Click on the "Register for Discounts" or reference the FAN account number 2717758


VERIZON WIRELESS DISCOUNT

1. Go to verizonwireless.com/renewdiscount
2. Select the 'Validate by Paystub' option. Complete the 'Employment Validation Form' including your account information and employer information with the business location. Click Continue.
3. Via the 'Upload Your Paystub' screen, attach your paystub. Please blackout any income or other sensitive information. Your name, company name and the date must be legible on the copy you submit.


SAM'S CLUB DISCOUNT

Eligible employees can receive $10 off their Sam's Club membership enrollment fee, or a $25 gift card after enrolling in a Sam's Plus membership. Contact your branch for more information.
